# Welcome to AI Pulse

**AI Pulse** is my running log of the most interesting things happening in AI right now —
new model releases, tools I want to try, papers I've read, and ideas worth keeping.

## How this works

Every entry here is just a markdown file in `src/AI-Pulse/`. To add a new one:

1. Create a file like `src/AI-Pulse/2026-06-20-my-topic.md`
2. Add the frontmatter block (title, date, tags, summary, emoji)
3. Write your notes in markdown — images dropped in the same folder work too

That's it. The new entry shows up here automatically. ✨

## What goes here

- 🧠 **New models** — capabilities, benchmarks, what changed
- 🛠️ **Tools & libraries** — things I want to experiment with
- 📄 **Papers** — short summaries and my takeaways
- 💡 **Ideas** — patterns and techniques worth revisiting

> Tip: keep entries short and skimmable. Future-me will thank present-me.
